A lightweight C++ Header only Library for Tensor Manipulations and Neural Networks!

RASH was born from a curiosity to deeply understand the internal workings of tensors, dynamic tensor definitions, and optimization functionalities. While it may not yet achieve the performance of production-grade libraries, it showcases smart and flexible design choices, enabling tensors to perform a wide range of operations with ease.
